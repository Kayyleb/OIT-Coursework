Stack & Queues

Stack: LiFo data structure
	Last in, First out
Push: Adding to top of the Stack
Pop: Returning, and Removing off of stack
Peek: Returning top of the stack (not removing)
	Same thing as "top"


Queue: FiFo data structure
	First in, First out
Enqueue: Adding to back of the queue
Dequeue: Removing from the front of the queue
Peek: Returning (not removing) from the front of the queue
	Same thing as "top"
